iSolutions
~~~~~~~~~~

iSolution is your IT technology support team, delivering university systems, software and services.

.. note::

    You can find out more information about the University's IT support `here <https://sotonac.sharepoint.com/teams/IT>`_.

1. Access IT support: visit https://go.soton.ac.uk/ithelp to find out opening time, locations, and contact details.
2. Create and manage your account

.. tabs::
    .. group-tab:: On a University Computer
        Log in using **subscribe** as the username and password, and follow the screen prompt.
    .. group-tab:: On Your Own Device
        Visit https://subscribe.soton.ac.uk, select the account setting you want to manage (e.g. reset your password), and follow the screen prompts.

3. Connect from anywhere: visit https://go.soton.ac.uk/remote to find out how to access the university systems and services from anywhere in the world.

.. important:: **Stay safe online**
    
    Check out https://go.soton.ac.uk/infosec to be aware of potential cyber treats and access advice on how to keep yourself and university information safe.

5. Connect to the Wi-Fi: The University's Wi-Fi network *eduroam* is available across campus and at Southampton General Hospital. You can log in using your University's credential (*username@soton.ac.uk* and password). Visit https://go.soton.ac.uk/wifi for guidance and configuration settings.

.. tip:: **How do I find a computer to use at the University?**

    Check out the University's `interactive campus maps <https://campusmaps.soton.ac.uk>`_ to find out the locations of approximately 2,000 computers across all campuses.

6. Download software: visit https://software.soton.ac.uk to download a wide range of programs and software that the University offers. You'll need to log in using your University's credential and password.
